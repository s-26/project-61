About Guru

Bimba Raghavendra is an acclaimed classical musician and a Veena exponent from Bangalore, in the Carnatic music genre. She is a performer, teacher and administrator. She has trained under Dr Suma Sudhindra, an acclaimed Veena artist in the country, for the past 40 years. Bimba is a Veena teacher/ coach/ mentor for many musical institutes across Bangalore with the most notable institute being Dr Suma Sudhindra’s Tarangini Institute where she has been teaching for over 40 years. In addition she also delivers online classes via Skype to her international students. Bimba has also performed at many prestigious events in India and abroad. When she is not teaching, she is either performing or managing back stage for many musical performances.
